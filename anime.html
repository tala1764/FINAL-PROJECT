~Banana Fish~

-With the anime released in 2018 after the long success this work had since the manga's release back in the 1980's, the anime show went viral as well! The story revolves around the main protagonist Ash Lynx who is a street gangster leader who uncovers a criminal conspiracy of "banana fish", a mysterious drug that brainwashes its users. Throughout, we join him on his adventures as he keeps investigating further into the case.



~Diabolik Lovers~

-This anime is about 6 vampire half-brothers who are offered sacrificial brides. Their most recent sacrifcial bride was Yui Komoro, who was distressed and had her blood drunk by them. We see her journey as she attempts to discover about their pasts, and why she had an exceptionally "delicious" blood, as the brothers claimed, making them addicted to her blood even more and involve in many fights with each other for her blood.

Who Am I?..

-This may not be a part of a show, but MISHO was very inspired by her shirt that happens to have become the title of the painting, "WHO AM I?"! Her shirt was crimson red and black, it had Mickey Mouse's head on it. For such, the colors of the room in the painting represent the shirt. Despite how deep the painting looks, it has no certain meaning or refereance to a certain theme, it is simply mysterious, this is becuase the shirt MISHO had had no certain emotion of happiness or saddness..It was and still is a mystery that shall never be resolved.
