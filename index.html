Who is MISHO?

-She is a Syrian artist who found her passion towards architecture and is currently a 2nd year college student! As her hobby, she has always been passionate about art and has been drawing since age 6. Throughout the years, her art skills have frequently developed to th point that no painting has the same quality as the other. Of course, MIOSH is only a nickname to maintain her identity anonymous for protection purposes.
